# Electronic Design Automation

Electronic Design Automation (EDA) tools, workflows, and technical studies that support integrated circuit design, semiconductor development, and device simulation.

---

## Overview

This section documents my study of Electronic Design Automation (EDA) technologies and design workflows during my undergraduate studies. It focuses on understanding how modern semiconductor devices are designed, simulated, verified, and manufactured using industry-standard EDA tools.

The materials collected here are intended to complement my research in semiconductor devices and MOSFET parameter extraction by providing a broader understanding of the semiconductor design ecosystem.
