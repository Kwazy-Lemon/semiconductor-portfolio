# Semiconductor Fundamentals

## Overview

This section documents my study of semiconductor device physics during my undergraduate years. It focuses on the fundamental concepts that form the basis of modern semiconductor devices, particularly MOSFETs and integrated circuits.

Rather than serving as a textbook, this collection summarizes the concepts, notes, and learning materials that have supported my research in MOSFET parameter extraction, compact modeling, and electronic design automation (EDA).

---

## Objectives

The goals of this section are to:

- Build a solid understanding of semiconductor device physics.
- Connect theoretical concepts with engineering applications.
- Establish the foundation for device characterization and MOSFET modeling.
- Support future research and technical projects.
