## Overview

In this paper, a novel technique is developed to estimate the parameters of Level-1 MOSFET compact models with systematic and consistent results at different biases. The consistency of parameter estimation at several biases is achieved through the use of an analytical approach combined with MATLAB programming for automatic data processing.
