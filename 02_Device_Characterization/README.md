# Device Characterization

Methods and techniques for analyzing the electrical behavior of semiconductor devices through current-voltage (I–V) and capacitance-voltage (C–V) characterization.

---

## Overview

This section documents my study of semiconductor device characterization techniques. It focuses on understanding how electrical measurements can be used to evaluate device performance and extract important physical parameters.

These studies provide the experimental and analytical foundation for my work on MOSFET parameter extraction and compact device modeling.
